How to Use This Roadmap

Read the Concept

Each day starts with tight bullet points. Read them first — they're the mental model you need before anything else.

Watch the Video

1–2 curated YouTube links per day. Watch at 1.25× speed. Pause when you need to — never rush comprehension.

Every concept has a practical mini-project. 20 lines of code beats 2 hours of passive watching every time.
